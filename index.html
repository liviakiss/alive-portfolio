About Me

Designing with clarity,
building with intention.

I'm Lívia — a product designer based in Europe, working with clients and companies worldwide. I think in systems. I care about the details most people skip. And I believe good design isn't just about how something looks — it's about how clearly it communicates, how intuitively it works, and how confidently it represents the people behind it. I founded ALIVE Design Studio to do exactly that — create digital experiences with real intention behind every decision.

My background combines UX/UI design with frontend development, grounded in cognitive psychology and system thinking. I don't just hand off files — I understand how designs get built, which means fewer surprises and better results on both sides.

When I'm not designing or coding, you'll find me learning languages, reading, gaming or picking apart the UX of things that really shouldn't have bad UX.

Currently open to freelance projects and full-time opportunities — especially with international teams and companies in Japan. 🇯🇵
